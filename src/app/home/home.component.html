Welcome to Missoni Baia,

Welcome to the Sweet Life.

Located in the fast-growing Edgewater neighborhood of Miami, Missoni Baia is the first- ever branded residences from Missoni, the iconic luxury fashion and design house founded in 1953. Missoni Baia Condominium, known as one of Miami’s most luxurious pre- construction condos, will be an impressive 200 feet of frontage on Biscayne Bay in East Edgewater providing the ultimate atmosphere for your enjoyment.

The first truly boutique residences in Edgewater Miami is developed by OKO Group, one of Europe’s most successful developers, designed by Internationally renowned Hani Rashid of Asymptote Architecture and inspired by Missoni, one of the most legendary fashion houses and most recognizable fashion labels in the world. Together they will CREATE an evolution of Edgewater and add Missoni Baia Residences to the breathtaking Miami Skyline. The Missoni Baia condominium will be a luxury waterfront tower with 56 floors and 229 condo residences. All units will offer spectacular views of Biscayne Bay and the Miami Skyline. The residences range from one bedroom, one bathroom up to four bedrooms, six bedrooms + den, all perfectly designed around the basic principal of full amenity and luxurious condominium while emphasizing low maintenance costs and move in ready residences. Fabulous gastronomy and nightlife await in Midtown, the Design District, and Wynwood. Professional success in the business core of Downtown Miami and Brickell, within minutes of South Beach. All the rhythm, energy, and appeal of the Magic City is yours.
